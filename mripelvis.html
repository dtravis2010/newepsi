Entity specific
MRI PELVIS WITHOUT CONTRAST MRI PELVIS is also used for SACRUM, COCCYX, SI JOINTS, RECTUM, ANUS, OVARIES, BLADDER and PROSTATE. Scheduled as MRI Pelvis with a note to indicate area of interest.

MRI Defecogram
  • Notes: Will now be transcribed as MRI Pelvis Without, similar to THP.
MRI PELVIS WITH AND WITHOUT CONTRAST Triple Contrast MRI Pelvis:
  • Contrasts: IV Contrast, Vaginal Contrast (US gel), Rectal Contrast (US gel).
  • Notes: Document "Triple Contrast MRI" in scheduling instructions.
MRI PELVIS WITH CONTRAST MRIS ARE RARELY DONE JUST WITH CONTRAST PLEASE OBTAIN REVISED ORDER FOR WITH AND WITHOUT. IF YOU HAVE QUESTIONS PLEASE CONSULT A SUPERVISOR
